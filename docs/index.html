01 / High Concept
What is Inkform?

Inkform is a 3D, cinematic-atmosphere stealth-puzzle game. The player controls a creature made of ink, escaping from the laboratory that cultivated it.

It can swallow different objects to gain corresponding abilities — swallow a remote to control giant machinery, swallow an anchor to sink underwater — using them to evade periodic scanning threats and solve the puzzles blocking its path.

Oppressive, lonely, curious all at once. Cold palette, strong light/shadow. No dialogue — emotion carried by rhythm and sound.

02 / Design Pillars
Core Philosophy

Swallow-to-Empower

Every ability is both a stealth tool and a puzzle key — one object, one solution.

Silent Stealth

Threats are "environmental pressure," not combat — evasion rhythm creates tension.

Environmental Storytelling

No dialogue; the story is told through scenery, light/shadow, and sound.

One Object, One Thought

Puzzles revolve around "what should I swallow right now," encouraging observation.

03 / Swallow-Ability System
Six Abilities

Remote Control

Swallow: Remote

Remotely control giant machinery — cranes, conveyors, gates, searchlight towers. Switch off scan-light towers, move giant cover to block sightlines.

Stealth Puzzle Machinery

Anchor

Swallow: Anchor

Body becomes heavy, can sink. Dive into underwater passages to dodge surface scans. Hold down weight switches, resist water currents.

Stealth Puzzle Weight

Teleport Beacon

Swallow: Beacon

Place a marker, teleport back at any time. Lure the Sweeper to one side, then vanish. Cross one-way break-points, operate mechanisms in two locations.

Stealth Puzzle Displacement

Bulb / Battery

Swallow: Bulb

Emit light or discharge into mechanisms. Light up dark segments, power unpowered mechanisms, activate light-sensitive switches.

Risk-Reward Power

Magnet

Swallow: Magnet

Magnetic, attracts metal. Drag a metal plate as movable cover blocking scan-light. Pull metal doors, grab keys, short circuits.

Cover Circuit

Balloon

Swallow: Balloon

Become light, float upward briefly. Float over ground pressure-sensor zones, rise to high cover, cross high walls.

Traversal Evasion

05 / Threat System
Binary Survival

Fixed Scanners

Rotating searchlights, sweeping laser grids, fixed cameras. Predictable scan cycles — time your passage or evade with an ability.

The Sweeper

A colossus that periodically passes through the area. Behavior loop: approach from afar → enter and scan → leave. The signature high-pressure source of the demo.

Touched by Red Light = Death

No health bar. No combat HP. The threat is binary: safe or dead. Immediate return to nearest checkpoint.

07 / Art & Audio
Direction

Visual

  • Low-saturation cold palette
  • Strong light/shadow contrast
  • Silhouette-driven composition
  • Scan-light as natural attention guide
  • Semi-transparent ink texture, malleable body
  • "Bulged out" transformation (Kirby Mouthful Mode reference)

Audio

  • Ambient sound dominant, strong spatial sense
  • Minimal or no musical score
  • Sweeper: low-frequency approach sound as "auditory radar"
  • Spotted moment: sharp, piercing feedback + camera impact
  • Reference tone: INSIDE / Somerville
  • Emotion carried by rhythm and sound, not dialogue
08 / Technical
Stack

Engine

Unity 6 (6000.1.17f1). URP 2D Renderer adapted for 3D pipeline. Rigidbody physics.

Architecture

Event bus S_GameEvent for decoupled broadcasts. ManagerRoot cross-scene persistence. Level Sections with S_LevelConfig. Input-lock pattern.

Milestones

M1: Movement + swallow + one scan threat + death/checkpoint.
M2: Core three abilities + puzzles.
M3: Segment abilities + art/audio + closing shot.

Escape Begins

A body of ink. A laboratory of cold light. One path out.
